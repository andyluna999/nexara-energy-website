# Nexara Energy Solutions — Brand Guidelines

> Tokens defined here are canonical. Additional tokens (components, motion, etc.) will be pulled from Figma as the project expands.

---

## Look & Feel

- Simple, straight to the point, easy to read
- Corporate optimism — think Microsoft mid-2010s (first Surface devices, Windows 8 era)
- **Flat design:** no gradients, no drop shadows, no 3D figures
- **No rounded corners** — everything sharp, cut, precise
- **Diagonal motifs** — angles and diagonal lines as a design language; feels dimensional, engineered, and directional
- Medium-high visual contrast (not extreme)
- Professional, modern, trustworthy
